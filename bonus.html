Neapolitan Mastiff

Neapolitan Mastiff is a sizeable dog, finding its place in many moments in history. The floppy and wrinkled pup may not seem that agile, but they are fairly confident, quick, and active.

  1. These dogs are highly protective of the owner families, giving very strong and fearless stares.
  2. Neapolitan Mastiffs are easily found in shelters and rescues at a lower price, though they can be adopted from breeders as well.
  3. These dogs do not have many needs when it comes to exercise. A bit of room at home and a little time on a leash for a walk is good enough for them.
